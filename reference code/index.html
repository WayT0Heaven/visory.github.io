Shia Muslims

Shia Islam is one of the two major denominations of Islam, the other being Sunni Islam. Shia Muslims believe that Ali, the cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet Muhammad, was his rightful successor and the first Shia Imam. The Shia community has a distinct set of religious practices and rituals, such as mourning the martyrdom of Ali's son, Hussain, during the Islamic month of Muharram. Despite their differences, both Shia and Sunni Muslims share the core beliefs of Islam, including the belief in one God and the Prophet Muhammad as his messenger.
